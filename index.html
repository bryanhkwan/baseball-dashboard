    How Scores Are Calculated
    Hitter score (0–100)
    • 32% On-base: How often they reach base (OBP)
    • 26% Power: How hard/far they hit (SLG)
    • 18% Contact: How consistent their hitting is (AVG)
    • 14% Discipline: Walks vs. strikeouts (BB:K)
    • 10% Speed: Stolen bases and baserunning
    Pitcher score (0–100)
    • 30% Run prevention: Keeps runs off the board (ERA)
    • 24% Traffic control: Limits runners on base (WHIP)
    • 20% Strikeouts: Gets batters out via strikeout (K/9)
    • 16% Command: Strikes vs. walks ratio (K:BB)
    • 10% Damage control: Prevents home runs (HR/9)
    Fit thresholds
    • Priority (80+): Top-tier fit — recruit immediately
    • Pursue (68–79): Strong candidate — make contact
    • Monitor (55–67): Developing player — track over the season
    • Depth (<55): Specialist or depth role — situation-dependent
